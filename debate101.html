What is Policy Debate?

Policy Debate is a team competition about solving real-world problems. You will:

  • Work with a partner on the Affirmative or Negative
  • Use evidence and logic to support your arguments
  • Give speeches and ask questions (cross-ex)
  • Try to convince the judge to vote for your team

The Stock Issues

  • Significance: Why the problem matters
  • Inherency: Why it isn't fixed now
  • Solvency: How the plan will solve the problem
  • Harms: What bad things happen if we don’t fix it
  • Topicality: Is the plan about the topic?

  5. 2AC

  7. 2NC
  8. Cross-Ex 4
  9. 1NR
  10. 1AR
  11. 2NR
  12. 2AR

Middle school speech times are usually shorter than high school — check with your coach for details!

How to Win

  • Answer every argument made by the other team
  • Explain why your world is better
  • Use strong evidence
  • Stay organized and speak clearly
  • Make eye contact with the judge

FAQ

What if I mess up?

It's okay! Just take a breath and keep going — judges know you’re learning.

What is prep time?

Prep time lets you talk with your partner before speeches. Use it wisely!

What do judges look for?

Clear arguments, solid evidence, confidence, and respect for your opponents.
